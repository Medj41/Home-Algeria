Welcome to Algeria

Algeria boasts breathtaking landscapes, a symphony of natural beauty that captivates the soul. The Sahara Desert unfolds like a golden tapestry, with vast dunes that dance in the sunlight, casting shadows that paint the landscape in warm hues. The lush Kabylie region is a mosaic of green mountains, adorned with charming villages that cling to the hillsides. Algeria's Mediterranean coastline is a serene blend of azure waters, pristine beaches, and historic ports, where history meets the sea. From the ancient casbahs of Algiers to the surreal landscapes of Tassili n'Ajjer, Algeria's scenery is an awe-inspiring testament to nature's artistry.

About Timgad

Timgad, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Algeria, is an ancient Roman city founded in the 1st century AD. Known for its well-preserved ruins, Timgad features a well-planned grid layout with impressive arches, a triumphal arch, and a Capitol. It offers a glimpse into the architectural and cultural heritage of Roman Africa.
